# New TMs (TM51–TM66)

This document covers the 16 TMs added in Modern Crystal, along with the original learnset methods that motivated each addition.

**Access key:**
- **Level** — the move appears in at least one Pokémon's natural level-up learnset
- **Egg** — the move is available as an egg/breeding move for at least one species

---

## Summary

| Category                | Count | TMs                              |
|-------------------------|-------|----------------------------------|
| Gen 1 TMs restored      | 6     | TM51–54, TM63–64                 |
| Brand new TMs           | 10    | TM55–62, TM65–66                 |
| Level-up access only    | 1     | TM53 (Thunder Wave)              |
| Level-up + egg access   | 15    | All others                       |

> **Note:** All 16 moves are learnable by at least one Pokémon via level-up in the base game. Adding them as TMs broadens access to Pokémon that fit the move's type or theme but couldn't otherwise learn it.
